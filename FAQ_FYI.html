For Your Information

  • You cannot replace the fried rice in the combo meals with young chow fried rice.

  • If you are ordering around 7-8 PM, IMMEDIETLY come pick up your order. WE WILL NOT WAIT FOR YOU!

  • The dine-in area is open, we just have the lights off.

  • We don't make egg drop soup ready on the go anymore because of it being the least ordered soup. If you're dining in and you want egg drop soup but it's too small of a portion, then we can't make it. (ex. 1 cup of egg drop soup is too small. multiple cups, small bowl, or large bowl of egg drop soup is good enough to make.)

  • Won ton soup, Hot and Sour soup, and pho are NOT included with the combo meals or meals when eating in. Soups are not included with ALL of the to-go meals.

  • Adding extra veggies/meat can be $2 or more.

  • There are NO REFUNDS after paying for your food!

  • if we got your order wrong, you can come back and replace it. if you had taken A BITE out of your food already, then we can't replace it.

  • If you order to-go inside the restuarant, it is reccommended that you pay first. You can also wait in your car and we'll bring your food to you only if you have paid first.

  • Silverware/chopsticks are not included with your to-go order. Please ask kindly if you need it, it is free.

  • You can pay on the phone.

  • We don't do delivery.

  • If you live at the Kearsly Retirement Apartments, we will delivery to you for free.

  • We don't do senior/military discounts.

  • We don't do soda fountain anymore. If you want a cup of ice with your drink, please let the waiter know.

  • Gift cards are back!
